Sarajevo

The capital of mountain-rugged Bosnia and Herzegovina, is a charming city reflecting perfect symbiosis of the past and the modern, a bridge between the East and the West. Along only a street you will come across a mosque, a synagogue, an orthodox and a catholic church. Sarajevo did become a symbol of the coexistence of communities from different cultural, ethnic and religious backgrounds.

Things to explore in Sarajevo

  • Walk among the pigeons at the Sebilj Fountain
  • Explore Baščaršija, Sarajevo’s Old Town
  • Enjoy a Bosnian Coffee
  • Watch the Vječna Vatra, The Eternal Flame
  • Watch the sunset at Žuta Tabija, The Yellow Fortress
  • Retrace the 1984 Winter Olympics
