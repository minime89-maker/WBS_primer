Zagreb

Modern capital city has a kind of small-town charm, with an expansive old hilltop district of cobblestone streets and squares lit to this day by gas lamps. By day you can amble the pedestrian streets and park yourself at one of the many cafes, and by night you can paint the town red and see why those in the know make such a fuss of Zagreb’s nightlife.

Things to explore in Zagreb

  • Old town developed as two separate hilltop settlements side-by-side
  • Gradec secular part of Zagreb’s medieval core, populated by artisans and tradesmen
  • Downtown Zagreb is perhaps the town's area where you will spend most of your time
  • First farmers's market and since then they are "the belly of the neighborhoods”.
  • The Zagreb Eye observation deck is a visual paradise for lofty view seekers.
