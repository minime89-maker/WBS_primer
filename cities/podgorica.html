Podgorica

As Montenegro’s capital, Podgorica is the country’s most important city. It’s its economic, cultural, educational, and political center and has a lot of historical and architectural value. However, what makes Podgorica such an attractive city is its downright gorgeous setting at the confluence of the Ribnica River and Morača River. This location is no coincidence; it’s been strategically important for centuries.

Things to explore in Podgorica

  • Experience Montenegro’s Niagara Falls
  • Hike To Various Viewpoints Around The City
  • Take A Dip In The Moraca River
  • Spend Time People Watching In The Parks
  • See The Remarkable Transformer-Like Robots
  • Crisscross Your Way To Millennium Bridge
